Reflectance Spectrum %





















To correct for the differences between instruments
and to move the measurements closer to what happens to the light.
Measurements of light reflectance is given as the percentage
or proportion of light (for each wavelength or color) that refelcts from the object.
To get a real reflectance value, we substracted the "dark voltage" from the measured voltages, and then divide the sample by the standard and cinvert to a percent value.
